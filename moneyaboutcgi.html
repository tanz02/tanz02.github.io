About Us.


Mike's Business Tips is a non profit organization looking to help small buisness owners for free. We don't ask for any sort of monetary payment or donation. We are simply looking to help out our local community business' that we do not wanna see dissapeear. Too many of these places are shutting down before the big corporations take over. Small business owners have to support themselves and their families and they contribute to the local economy. If multiple small business fall down then there is a ripple in the local economy. This website was created to prevent any losses to our communities.

Who we are


We are a virtul organization with no physical address. All our resources are utilized on the web. Our organization was founded in 2016. This site was made in order to help people due to the fact that we have such a weak economy today.
